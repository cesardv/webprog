Welcome


Hi, my name is Cesar Velez, and I'm a senior Computer Science major at Georgia State University.
Originally a mathematics major, about midway through my undergraduate studies I decided to go binary and into practical application of discrete mathematics, aka Computer Science.
I like to tinker with things, build things, take things apart thus I sort of got bored with all the math theory with so little application!

I was originally born in Cali, Colombia but I have been living in Atlanta, Georgia for almost 15 years now. However, everytime I go back to Colombia to visit I always want to stay! It's just very beautiful there, the people are very warm, and the food is amazing... despite the country's economic and political problems.

I enjoy learning about computer science and the latests and newests web technologies. Apart from learning programming languages, I also like learning new “natural” languages. I speak fluent Spanish, English and pretty decent French. I’ve also dabbled in Russian, Italian, and Portuguese!
I also enjoy playing guitar, which I've been making noise with since the 8th grade, playing PS3, and watching movies.
